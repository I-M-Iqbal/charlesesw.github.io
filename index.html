Final Year Project:

For my Final Year Project at the University of Birmingham I created a hardware trigger generator for the Universal Serial Bus. This aimed to show the vulnerabilities of the USB protocol and focused on keyboard signals. The hardware used is a Raspberry Pi Pico, and written in C. It is open sourced on Github and if you have any inquiries please send them to fyp@charliew.net. I would also like to give special thank yous to my ringmates Sofia, Imaan, Fern and Anna for proofreading my paper.


RateMyModule:

This was the website I made for my Team Project module at university, designed to allow other uni students to review the modules they had taken, to help other students make informed decisions about optional module choices.


Pulsifi:

This was a social media I worked on as a part of Durhack 2022, where we added a twist that a post would be deleted when a timer runs out, but the timer gets longer and shorter with likes and dislikes. We believed this would be a good way to democratise social media.


Lost In The Snow:

This was a text based adventure game I created in Haskell during Durhack 2024 with Ella Gardner.
